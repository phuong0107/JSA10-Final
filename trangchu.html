WELLCOME TO TEAM VIETNAM


Hi, we are Team Vietnam and this is our story. After FIRST Global Challenge 2017, we come back to our country and build Vietnam STEAM Union – a community for students across Vietnam that have passion for STEAM. We organize Vietnam STEAM Challenge and many workshops to ignite students’ passion for Robotics.

During the 6 years since Team Vietnam’s establishment, each generation of our team has always strived to do activities with the aim of building a solid STEAM community in Vietnam, spreading the fire of passion to all students around Vietnam. Team Vietnam will continue to contribute and create more values for the community.

As our slogan is “Ablaze like a golden star”, we are willing to make effort both in the performance at FGC and in inspiring Vietnamese teenagers. Therefore, we recently created the School Tour to bring STEAM and our passion to students across Vietnam. Moreover, we are continuing to organize Vietnam STEAM Challenge (VSC) and Vietnam Robotics Challenge (VRC) to create a playground for those students so they can exchange and learn. Besides, we would like to bring our burning passion for STEAM and our willingness to inspire out to the global community. We strongly believe that age, gender, and circumstances will not stop you from achieving your dreams.
