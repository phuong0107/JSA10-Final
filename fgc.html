FIRST GLOBAL CHALLENGE



Every year, FIRST Global organises an international Olympic-style robot competition called FIRST Global Challenge. A team of high school students representing a country will be invited to attend. It promotes STEM education and careers for youth and was created by Dean Kamen in 2016 as an expansion of FIRST, an organization with similar objectives.
