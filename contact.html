Team Vietnam 2023

After the success of the Vietnam Robotics Challenge (VRC) – a nationwide robotics competition, 9 members from various regions across Vietnam were selected carefully to be Team Vietnam FGC 2023. They are students from six different high schools located not only in urban areas but also in rural ones. Taking part in the FIRST Global Challenge, we want to express our progressive, exchanging, and learning spirit. We believe that, together, we can be the change to change the world and solve our planet’s problems.
